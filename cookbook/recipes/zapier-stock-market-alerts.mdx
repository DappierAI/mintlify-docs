title: "Automate Stock Market Alerts via Email Using Zapier and Dappier"
---

Dappier’s **Stock Market Data Model** allows you to fetch **real-time stock prices** based on user queries and automate responses via **Zapier and Gmail**. This guide walks you through creating a **Zap** that:

- Detects **stock-related queries** via **email**.
- Extracts the **stock ticker** using **Zapier Formatter**.
- Fetches **real-time stock market data** using **Dappier’s Stock Market Model**.
- Sends the **stock price update via email**.

## **Prerequisites**

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A **[Zapier account](https://zapier.com)**
- A **Dappier API Key** (Generate it from [Dappier Platform](https://platform.dappier.com) under **Settings > Profile > API Keys**)
- A **Gmail account** (or any Zapier-supported email service)

---

## **Step 1: Set Up a Trigger for Email Queries**

1. **Log in to Zapier** and click **Create a Zap**.
2. **Select a Trigger**:
   - Search for **"Gmail"** and select it.
   - Choose **"New Email Matching Search"** as the trigger event.
3. **Set up the search criteria**:
   - Enter a keyword like **"Stock Price"** to filter relevant emails.
4. Click **Continue**, then **Test Trigger**.

---

## **Step 2: Extract Stock Symbol Using Zapier Formatter**

1. Click the **"+"** icon to add an action.
2. Search for **"Formatter by Zapier"** and select it.
3. Choose **"Text"** as the action event.
4. Select **"Split Text"** to extract the stock ticker.
5. Configure the fields:
   - **Input**: Select the **email body** from Step 1.
   - **Separator**: Space (" ")
   - **Segment Index**: Choose **the stock ticker position** (e.g., the second word in “Stock Price TESLA”).
6. Click **Continue**, then **Test the Step** to verify extraction.

---

## **Step 3: Fetch Real-Time Stock Price Using Dappier**

1. Click the **"+"** icon to add another action.
2. Search for **"Dappier"** and select it.
3. Choose **"Get Stock Market Data"** as the action event.
4. Configure the fields:
   - **Stock Symbol**: Use the extracted **ticker from Step 2**.
5. Click **Continue**, then **Test the Step** to retrieve the stock price.

---

## **Step 4: Send Stock Price Alert via Email**

1. Click the **"+"** icon to add another action.
2. Search for **"Gmail"** and select it.
3. Choose **"Send Email"** as the action event.
4. Configure the fields:

   - **Recipient**: `{{User Email}}`
   - **Subject**: `"Stock Market Update for {{Stock Symbol}}"`
   - **Body**:

     ```plaintext
     Hello,

     Here is the latest stock market update for {{Stock Symbol}}:

     - **Stock Price**: {{Dappier Stock Price Output}}

     Stay informed and make smarter investment decisions!

     Best,
     [Your Stock Market Assistant]
     ```

5. Click **Continue**, then **Test the Step**.

---

## **Final Step: Publish Your Zap**

1. Click **Publish Zap** to enable **automated stock market updates**.
2. Your Zap will now **run whenever a stock query email is received**, fetching stock prices and sending email updates.

---
