**Overview**

The Dappier Crawler user agent is `Dappier/1.0 (+https://docs.dappier.com/crawler; support@dappier.com)`. If you made it to this page, you may have noticed it in your server logs and wondered "what is this?".

**What is the Dappier Crawler?**

The Dappier crawler is used to index and collect trusted, approved Dappier customer website content. This data is used as input for training the models created by our client to power a variety of client-approved and configured use cases in the Dappier platform.

**Why is the Dappier Crawler Visiting My Site?**

We received the URL via one of our clients in the Dappier Platform (https://platform.dappier.com).  

If you have any questions or concerns about the crawler, please contact `support@dappier.com`.
