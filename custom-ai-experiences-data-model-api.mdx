---
title: 'Custom AI Experiences with your own data model API'
---
After configuring your AI Agent, it seamlessly transforms into a structured data model/RAG API, enabling smooth interactions with other AI applications. This transformation enables the creation of powerful and actionable insights, leveraging the collective intelligence of various AI systems.

<img
  className="block"
  src="/images/Data_Model.png"
  alt="Data Model"
/>

- Navigate to your **Data Model** tab of the AI Agent that you’ve created.
- Utilize the provided **API endpoint** or **Open AI schema - GPTs** to seamlessly integrate your newly created data model with other AI applications, unlocking endless possibilities for collaboration and innovation in the AI ecosystem.
- Additionally, utilize this data model to publish it on **Dappier’s RAG marketplace**, expanding its reach and accessibility to a broader user base.
